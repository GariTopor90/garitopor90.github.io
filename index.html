GuardPilot

Autonomous Security Drone for Smart Surveillance

About the Project

GuardPilot is an autonomous drone-based security solution designed for 24/7 monitoring of industrial and private areas. It offers a smarter, safer, and more cost-effective way to secure your perimeter.

Key Features

- Autonomous patrolling with real-time alerts
- Intrusion detection and facial recognition
- Controlled via secure mobile/web application
- Works day and night, in all weather conditions

Benefits

Save costs on human security, reduce response time, and ensure reliable surveillance coverage with our intelligent drone technology.
